Biography

I am now a graduate student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and I received my undergraduate education at Harbin Engineering University (HEU), where I lay a solid foundation in wireless communication and signal processing-related areas, with both theoretical and practical ability. I ranked 2/361 (scored 91.11/100) and was recommended to further study at USTC. In USTC, I was lucky enough to be in the Prof. Zhengyuan Xu’s group and started my research in optical wireless communication field. My previous research experiences are somehow pretty wide: I spent the first one year focusing on physical layer of visible light communication and real-time communication system; then I tried to extend CNN object detection task to optical camera communication scenario and during this whole COVID-19 work-from-home period, I did some work of vision-based optical positioning.
